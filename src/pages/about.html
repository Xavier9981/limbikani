Hey, I'm Xavier K Muneku!

Trying to do my part in making Zambia and the world at large a better place.

Founder of Mabonzo

As a Zambian it hurts me to see the minimal original content that is on the internet representing us Zambians, the funny part is that even the minimal content present on the internet right now isn't even published by Zambians most of the time. Henceforth the content delieved isn't fact checked thoroughly and the stories told aren't told from a common Zambian's perspective.

That's why I'am doing my part with the setting up of Mabonzo a proudly Zambian owned website which is purposefully built to be an online encyclopedia of Zambian names only. My hope and prayer is that this initiative will help movitate more Zambians to do the same and maybe we can increase the content present on the net representing Zambians.

Glad you've reached this bottom end and I'm intrigued to hear from you so get in touch with me on WhatsApp and let me know how we can help improve Zambia's representation on the internet.
